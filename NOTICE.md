# Notices

## Tatoeba Sentence Data

The sentence pairs in `sentence-data.js` are sourced from Tatoeba and redistributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 France license.

Each sentence row retains:

- `source`: the data source name.
- `sourceId`: the Tatoeba Mandarin sentence ID.
- `translationId`: the Tatoeba English sentence ID.

Source: https://tatoeba.org/

License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/fr/

## CC-CEDICT Word Data

The compact pinyin and word-gloss entries in `word-data.js` are derived from CC-CEDICT for the Chinese words used in the local sentence bank.

Source: https://www.mdbg.net/chinese/dictionary?page=cc-cedict

License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

## New HSK Vocabulary Quiz Data

Vocabulary membership, official ordering, and HSK 1–3 level assignments in `data/hsk-2025-vocabulary-1-3.json` and `vocab-data.js` are derived from the November 2025 revised HSK examination syllabus, effective July 2026.

Official source: https://hsk.cn-bj.ufileos.com/3.0/%E6%96%B0%E7%89%88HSK%E8%80%83%E8%AF%95%E5%A4%A7%E7%BA%B21219.pdf

English definitions, pinyin spacing, alternate readings, and numeric pinyin are enriched from complete-hsk-vocabulary.

Source: https://github.com/drkameleon/complete-hsk-vocabulary
