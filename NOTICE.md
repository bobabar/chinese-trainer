# Notices

## Tatoeba Sentence Data

The sentence pairs in `sentence-data.js` are sourced from Tatoeba and redistributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 France license.

Each sentence row retains:

- `source`: the data source name.
- `sourceId`: the Tatoeba Mandarin sentence ID.
- `translationId`: the Tatoeba English sentence ID.

Source: https://tatoeba.org/

License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/fr/

## CC-CEDICT Word Data

The compact pinyin and word-gloss entries in `word-data.js` are derived from CC-CEDICT for the Chinese words used in the local sentence bank.

Source: https://www.mdbg.net/chinese/dictionary?page=cc-cedict
